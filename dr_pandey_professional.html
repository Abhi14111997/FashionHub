Frequently Asked Questions

Helping You Understand Your Care

Who is an Orthopedic Specialist and what do they treat? +
An orthopedic specialist diagnoses and treats conditions affecting the bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons. This includes fractures, joint pain, arthritis, sports injuries, spinal problems, and more. Dr. Pandey specializes in both surgical and non-surgical orthopedic care.
When should I see an Orthopedic Doctor? +
You should see an orthopedic doctor if you have persistent joint or bone pain, limited range of motion, swelling around a joint, pain after an injury, difficulty walking, or if you're experiencing back or spine pain that doesn't improve with rest. Early consultation leads to better outcomes.
What types of joint replacement surgeries are performed? +
Dr. Pandey performs knee replacement, hip replacement, and shoulder replacement surgeries. He uses minimally invasive techniques wherever possible to reduce surgical trauma, minimize blood loss, and accelerate the recovery process so patients can return to normal activities sooner.
How do I book an appointment with Dr. Pandey? +
You can book an appointment by filling the form on this page, calling us directly on +91 9580500147, or messaging us on WhatsApp. OPD hours are Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM and 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7.
Is physiotherapy required after orthopedic surgery? +
Yes, physiotherapy is an essential part of orthopedic recovery. It helps restore strength, flexibility, and mobility after surgery or injury. Dr. Pandey provides structured, personalised rehabilitation programs that are tailored to each patient's specific condition and recovery goals.
